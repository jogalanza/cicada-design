About Cicada

Founded in 2022, Cicada Design & Build brings fresh energy and modern approaches to construction while maintaining time-honored craftsmanship standards. Our lean structure allows us to deliver exceptional value without compromising quality.

Though new as a company, our team members each bring 10+ years of specialized experience in their respective trades. We combine this deep expertise with innovative techniques to deliver results that exceed expectations.
